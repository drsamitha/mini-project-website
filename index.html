Who We Are

Sip Piyasa, which was established in the year 2000, has been able to guide a large number of students to university opportunities. We are committed to continue to provide the necessary guidance.


Our Vition

Our vision is to become an academic supporter to work for the overall development of students so that they can strengthen talented and young minds and fly in the sky to achieve the best in their lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality education to every student and ensure his/her success. We commit ourselves to excellent education and cherish the tutors who are fully dedicated and committed to instilling trust and compassion between teachers and students and ensuring that the students will achieve all their academic goals.
